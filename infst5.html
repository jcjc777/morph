Infinite Star


An Absolute Beginner! After reading about 3D printing over the years, Infinite Star was curious about what the industry encompasses. He joined the class without any design experience and with very minimal tech savviness. Thus, he encountered much frustrations in mouse and software functions, not to mention seemingly road blocks with design progression. Determination and focus had allowed him to achieve his designs goals with the aid of countless on-line videos on software functions.

A lot of time was spent reconfiguring his design elements. There was no smooth path to each design, so much so that some designs were changed or altered as many as 30 times before meeting printable criterias. He now find it rather fun to work with "irregular" shapes, a term suggested by the instructor at the beginning of the course.

Since the end of the course, he had went on to work on most of his designs that was unsuccesful during the course. As a result, he now has a few finished printable designs in each category of the course requirements. He also like to acknowledge his course mates, Scharfer Stürmer and DD Glow for their inspirations and class assistances. As a person that does not ever draw, he is now greatly astonished to have completed a sizeble portfolio featured on this page and the Gallery.
