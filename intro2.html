A Brief Introduction

3D Modeling

Since 3D designing covers a few categories, the focus of this site is on 3D Modeling, creation of an object which has 3 dimensions (height, width, depth) in a virtual environment with the goal of having it printed (as opposed to 3D Rendering.) While the technology had been around for more than 30 years, it had only been used for broad applications within the past decade. It's advancement as a big consumer technology had somehow stagnated but it is evolving to include a wide range of professional enterprise applications across various industries, like aerospace, automotive, medical, retail, military/defense, and other professional services. The growth of industrial applications for 3D modeling and printing are projected to be about 18% per year over the next 5 years.
