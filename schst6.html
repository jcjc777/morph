Scharfer Stürmer


An avid soccer fan, Scharfer Stürmer has an engineering background with experience in CAD Programs for the construction industry but not small 3D designs. He is always interested in working with new softwares and enrolled in the course to get exposure to 3D modeling and printing. Aside from that, he also wanted design skills to complement a drafting course he was also pursuing.

He thinks 3D modeling and printing are still at their infancy and project a broad application in the future in the home construction industry with printed parts for home building.

The most difficult part of the experience for him was to find a shape or design that he could succesfully create for printing. According to him, the programs wasn’t so difficult to learn because of his past experiences and skills but at the same time also understands the frustrations that other course participants encounter in the design process. Therfore, he has a tip for all; “Start with simple shapes and understand how the program works. After every design you will getting better and better!”

Do check out his portfolio and more of his designs in the Gallery.
